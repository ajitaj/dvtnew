Features

 Class Room

The college has fully air- conditioned, specious & architecturally designed class rooms. It also has glossy black boards & flannel boards, furnished with adequate modern furniture

Library – cum - reading room

Books are life blood of any educational system. They are treasure of knowledge. So, to encourage and develop reading habit, well equipped library has been set up with the excellent collection of books including variety of encyclopedia and reference books, educative & informative leading journals and magazines, CD ROM. It is being continuously expanded

 Health & Physical Resource centre

‘A sound mind lives in a sound body’. The college has a fully furnished health & Physical resource centre to provides opportunity for students to indulge in indoor and outdoor sports activity. The students can avail of indoor games like chess, Carom board & outdoor games like badminton, volley ball, cricket etc.

 Science & Math Lab

Science is not only knowledge but also an art. This art is explored, experimented and practiced in Science resource centre. The college has a Science lab. Which is well equipped with all the necessary scientific apparatus. The College has also a mathematics lab having models, charts, graphs and pictures, concreted objects to demonstrate, explain and reinforce abstract mathematical ideas

 Psychology Labb

We have a curiosity to know intellectual level, Interests, aptitude, attitude, personality etc. The college has sufficient psychological apparatus, tests which are related to various aspects of human development. The beauty of this lab is to understand human behavior in its totality.

 ICT Lab

Our ICT lab has sufficient computers with the latest configuration, LAN connected with internet facility. Additionally it has printer, scanner, OHP, TV, DVD – CD Player, cordless microphones which are often used in the teaching learning process & make it effective.

 LANGUAGE LAB

A well developed & equipped language lab has already been set up for the personality

 Art & Craft and Music Lab

‘Art is soul of life & creativity’. A well equipped art & craft centre is established in the college. The centre is provided with musical instrument, such as harmonium, table, manjira and other indigenous instruments, sound system and many numbers of tools & equipment for various form of craft work

Multipurpose Hall

The college has hi-tech multipurpose hall. There is adequate arrangement of seating capacity of more than 200 persons to organize the meeting/events/seminar etc.

 Canteen

Canteen facility is also available in college campus. Good quality foods are available in suitable price.

Transportation

College provides transport facility to students and staff from important pick points of nearly area to the college campus at nominal charges. It also helps in educational tours & medical emergencies.

 Wi-Fi facility

The college has Wi-Fi campus with 24 hours broadband internet facility.
