Eligibility for Admission

Eligibility for admission to B.Ed course shall be as per NCTE norms i.e, candidate who have passed bachelor/ Master degree in Art/ Science/ Commerce, Securing 50% marks or above from a UGC Recognized University. Relaxation of marks for reserved category will be as per the govt. rule.
Note: - Admission shall be taken on merit basis as per marks (career point) obtain from secondary level to onwards.
